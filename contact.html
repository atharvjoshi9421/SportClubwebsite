Common Questions

Support Center

Do you offer day passes or trial sessions?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 1-day pass for local residents. For travelers, day passes are available for $25. Please contact our front desk to book your slot.

Can I freeze my membership?

Absolutely. Memberships can be frozen for up to 3 months per calendar year for medical or travel reasons with a written notice 7 days in advance.

Are personal trainers included in the membership?

General floor staff are available to assist. However, dedicated 1-on-1 Personal Training packages are sold separately or included in our 'Elite' membership tier.
